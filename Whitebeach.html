Basdaku White Beach

Basdaku Beach Resort is its popular name and considered one of the best beaches in Cebu. The name "basdaku" (or "basdakoo) is the Cebuano (Visayan language) transliteration for the word ‘big sand’ (bas for sand, daku for big or wide).
